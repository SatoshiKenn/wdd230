Discover Estacion Central!

Estacion Central has one of the most crowded places of Santiago because of the train station and the bus terminal.

Estacion Central has a fountain and is really common to have a lot of street vendors

Estacion Central has a lot of fast food restaurants, because is a really crowded place is big target to all fast food restaurants

At the same time Estacion Central has a lot of entrepreneurs, like the fast food chain restaurants the people who lives near to Estacion Central or into the comuna know about the great potential it has to sell food, that is why a lot of people have their own different type of restaurants.

As you can see in this picture some entrepreneurs have the opportunity to place big locals and they chose Estacion Central as one of their targets because of the location and what I said before, theres a lot of people always transit there!

In Estacion Central we have a lot of foreign food restaurants, one of the most visited is "El Cacique" that sells Peruvian food, is really tasty and when you pass by the restaurant even with the pandemic situation they are always full of customers!

The entrepreneurs got creative and try to use different methods to attract customers to their restaurants! As we can see here this restaurant is painted with some food ingredients.

Here's an aerial picture of one place that is really crowded all the time! General Velazquez and Alameda streets have a lot of traffic from vehicles and people!

Also Santiago has a subway, so in Estacion Central we have one of the train terminal stations and also a metro station called Estacion Central.
