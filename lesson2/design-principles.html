Contrast

Crunchyroll

Crunchyroll

Crunchyroll has a good contrast design, it helps to emphasize the series you can watch on the platform. The orange details help to navigate into the site and to identify the different elements to interact.

White Space and Clean Design

GitHub

GitHub

The GitHub site has a clean design, the white spacing between the elements helps a lot to have good readability. The clean design helps you to recognize the different sections of the site without problems. With this kind of design, we can achieve a professional site.

PARC: Repetition

W3Schools

W3Schools

W3Schools has on their home page a lot of repetition. I only show two of the sections but most of their different tutorials have the same style, they just change the colors and the title but it's the same design for each section.
