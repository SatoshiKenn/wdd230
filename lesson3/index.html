Site Name

For the domain name, I chose foodadvisorset.cl because, in this way, I can attract the attention of the different businesses that want to know about the local restaurants in our comuna (in Chile is an administrative division not related to neither determined by urban areas).

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to help new restaurants, food trucks, and other kinds of food businesses to have a foundation of how food businesses work in our comuna, help them to localize a good place to establish their business, help them to learn how to create good advertising and finally help them with our connections to make their business more known in the comuna. Help visitors to know and visit the local restaurants giving them recommendations about the different restaurants in the comuna.
