Target Audience

The target audience will be owners of food businesses, people who want to establish their restaurant or to start a new one. People who live in the comuna and visit the comuna that want to try new restaurants and they will be able to find information about all the restaurants that work with the chamber.

Personas

Maria Guzman, she wants to start a new food restaurant, she is investigating about the different places she could use to put her food truck, she moved two months ago with her family and wants to implement the same business she had in her last comuna. She's 45 years old. She only has her High school education, she already has social networks but didn't work too well in her last comuna, she had success because she knew a lot of people and that helped her to have more clients. Her motivation to visit the program is all the connections we have to do good advertising, also she wants to learn how to use her social networks to reach people by internet.

Jorge Ramirez. He has a family tradition of trying new restaurants, they already tried almost every restaurant in his comuna. He's currently in High school. He's 17 years old. His motivation to visit our site is to have information about new food businesses in his comuna to try with his family. He often looks for different restaurants on social networks but he realized that a lot of local restaurants don't have social networks.

Scenarios

I want to understand better how to use social networks with my food business.

We already visit a lot of restaurants and we want to try new food.

I want to know if a international type of food will work to start a new business here.

I just moved to Estacion Central and I want to know strategic places to use my food truck.

What can I do if I want to have more clients? Do you have any advertising plans to help businesses?
